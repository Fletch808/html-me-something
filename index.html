My journey:    There and back again

Not all who wander are lost


There...

  • Cincinnati, Ohio

    • I spent my early years just outside Cincy.   Just a country boy at heart.


  • Los Angeles, California

    • In my 20's I was ready for life in the fast lane.   Bright lights, big city.


  • Salt Lake City, Utah

    • In my 30's I got married and settled down.   Life in the slow lane.


  • Cleveland, Ohio

    • Back to school and my son is born in 1998.   Things are about to change.


  • The island of Maui

    • Time for a change.   Sun, sand, and the island lifestyle.


  • South island of New Zealand

    • Time for an even bigger change.   Life down under.


    ...And back again.


Funny how life has a way of bringing things around full circle...
